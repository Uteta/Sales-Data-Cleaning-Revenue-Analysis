# Sales Data Insights

## Overview
This project analyzes cleaned sales datato extract revenue-based insights.

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: Calculated from Quantity * Price
- Average Order Value
- Top Performing Products by Revenue

## Findings
- Widget C generated the highest revenue
- Revenue Increases significantly with higher quantity purchases.
- Sales activity is consistent accross the month.

## Business Recommendations
- Promote high-revenue products.
- Introduce bundle discounts.
- Monitor daily revenue trends.
